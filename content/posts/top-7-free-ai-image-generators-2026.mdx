## 💡 Pakistani Users Ke Liye Critical Tips

### Tip 1: Sign Up Method Important Hai

- **Google sign-in** preferred (fastest)
- **Microsoft account** for Bing + Designer (one account, two tools)
- **Avoid Apple sign-in** — Pakistan mein recovery mushkil

### Tip 2: VPN Generally Nahi Chahiye

Saare 7 tools Pakistan se directly accessible hain. **Sirf MidJourney aur Stable Diffusion API** mein VPN ki zaroorat hai (jo paid hain).

### Tip 3: Prompts English Mein Likho

Roman Urdu prompts bhi chalte hain, lekin English mein **20x better** results milte hain.

**Bad prompt**: `"Aik larka coding kar raha hai"`

**Good prompt**: `"A young Pakistani man coding on a laptop in a dark room, neon lights, cinematic lighting, photorealistic, 4k"`

### Tip 4: Style Keywords Yaad Karo

Result quality **massively** depend karti hai in keywords pe:

- `"photorealistic, 4k, cinematic lighting"` — real photos ke liye
- `"digital art, vibrant colors, illustration"` — artwork
- `"minimal, flat design, vector"` — UI/logos
- `"oil painting, baroque style"` — fine art

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes Avoid Karein

<Callout type="warning" title="Ye Mat Karna">
1. **Free credits abuse karna** — multiple accounts ban karwa sakte hain
2. **Real persons ke faces generate karna** — ethical aur legal issues
3. **Copyright characters** (Marvel, Disney) — terms violation
4. **Hateful/violent content** — instant ban
5. **NSFW prompts** — har platform pe banned, account permanent disable
</Callout>
